NeoOta LLC

Media Data Infrastructure & Engineering

NeoOta LLC is a software engineering company focused on the aggregation, processing, and delivery of Japanese-language media data systems. Our infrastructure is designed to enable structured access to high-frequency media updates, metadata, and release information.

Core Engineering Areas:

  • Data Ingestion & Infrastructure: Engineering resilient pipelines for real-time aggregation of Japanese-language source data and metadata.
  • Data Structuring & Normalization: Designing systems for organizing, indexing, and standardizing media data for downstream applications.
  • Personalized Information Delivery: Developing subscription-based systems for tracking and notifying users of relevant media releases and updates.

U.S. Operations | Japan-focused systems expansion (planned 2027)
